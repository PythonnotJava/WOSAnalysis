AB In this paper, an efficient method to compute the phase impedance of a power distribution line is presented. This paper's main interest is to collect and analyze the impedance-temperature profiles in distribution lines by employing real-time phasor measurement units (PMUs) voltage and current measurements. The monitoring system has been developed for microgrids integrating ocean energies within the scope of the Mexican Ocean Energy Innovation Centre (CEMIE-Oceano), contributing to marine energy tools' innovation and development. The development tool can easily be applied to other distribution network components or to monitor microgrids with renewable-energy generation.
